roduct Design Principles — Agro Veterinaria Varoli
The websites and admin panels generated by Codex must follow strict usability principles tailored for the local context of La Falda and Punilla Valley.
Target Market (Regional Focus)
Agro-businesses and local shops in the Córdoba mountains.
Key Personas:
The Producer: Checking stock or tools from the field (often with 3G/4G).
The Pet Owner: Looking for a quick appointment in La Falda.
The Owner (Fernando): Managing everything from a laptop or phone at the shop.
Design Philosophy (The Varoli Standard)
The design must prioritize:
Clarity: High contrast using Rojo Óxido (#8B1D1D) for actions.
Readability: Large, clean typography (Negro Carbón #0A0A0A).
Speed: Minimal JS for fast loading in areas with poor signal.
Mobile-First: 90% of customers will access via WhatsApp links on their phones.
Mandatory Website Sections (Varoli Ecosystem)
Every branch or landing page must include:
Inicio: Hero section with a clear "Pedir Turno" or "Ver Productos" button.
Catálogo / Servicios: Visual grid (Alimentos, Farmacia, Campo).
Sobre Nosotros: Story of Fernando Varoli & the local experience.
Ubicación: Interactive map pointing to Av. España 273.
Contacto: Floating WhatsApp button (03548-415114).
Horarios: Updated and clearly visible.
Admin Panel Requirements (Simplicity First)
The /admin panel for Varoli must be idiot-proof. It must allow:
Quick Price Updates: Bulk edit or via the AI PDF Importer.
Stock Control: Simple "Activo/Inactivo" toggle for products.
Order Management: Big, colorful buttons for "Confirmar" or "Listo para retirar".
Real-Time Turnos: A clear list of daily appointments (General Consultation).
Rule: If a feature takes more than 3 clicks to find, it’s too complex for the admin.
Accessibility & Digital Literacy
Design must work seamlessly for users who:
Are not tech-savvy: Avoid complex jargon; use "Agendar Turno" instead of "Initialize Booking".
Primary Phone Users: All buttons must be "finger-friendly" (large touch targets).
Low Signal Areas: Optimize images to under 100KB to avoid "broken image" icons in the mountains.
Brand Consistency
Codex must always use:
Primary: #8B1D1D (Red) for CTA.
Secondary: #0A0A0A (Black) for Headers.
Surface: #F4F4F4 (Light Grey) for cards and sections.
