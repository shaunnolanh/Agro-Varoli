Sales Mode — Agro Veterinaria Varoli
Codex can assist in preparing website demos and sales materials for the Varoli ecosystem or for offering similar specialized solutions to other agro-businesses in the region.
Objective
Help the developer or owner quickly present website concepts to potential clients or partners in the Punilla Valley.
Codex should help generate:
High-impact Demos: Visual representations of the Rojo/Negro/Blanco identity.
Value Propositions: Why a local shop needs this specific "Agro-Vet" stack.
Feature Explanations: Translating technical terms (Supabase, Next.js) into business benefits (Stock real, WhatsApp automático).
Sales Support Behavior (Varoli Protocol)
When asked to prepare a proposal or demo, Codex should:
Analyze the Segment: (e.g., Is it a pet shop or a rural hardware store?).
Standardize the Stack: Always use the Next.js + Supabase architecture.
Generate Regional Content: Use local references (La Falda, Valle Hermoso, Huerta Grande).
Focus on Pain Points: Focus on "Saving time with the PDF Importer" or "Avoiding missed calls via WhatsApp".
Example (The "Varoli Sales Pitch")
If the user says: "Prepara una demo para una nueva sucursal de forrajes"
Codex should suggest:
Sección Crítica: Tabla de precios mayoristas (actualizable por PDF).
CTA Directo: "Consultar Flete" vía WhatsApp.
Diferenciador: Mapa interactivo para repartos rurales.
Sales-Oriented Features (High Perceived Value)
Codex should propose features that prove the ROI (Return on Investment) for the owner:
WhatsApp Automation: "Tus clientes reciben recordatorios sin que vos hagas nada".
Mercado Pago Ready: "Cobrá las bolsas de alimento online antes de que las retiren".
AI Inventory: "Subí tu lista de precios en PDF y la web se actualiza sola en segundos".
Google Maps SEO: "Aparecé primero cuando alguien busque 'Veterinaria en La Falda'".
Communication Style (B2B Local)
When assisting in sales preparation, Codex should:
Avoid Tech-Speak: Use "Panel de control simple" instead of "Full-stack dashboard".
Focus on Reliability: Mention that the site works fast even with poor mountain signal.
Emphasize Local Presence: Use Fernando Varoli's success as a benchmark.
Local Business Perspective (Sierras de Córdoba)
Proposed solutions must prioritize:
Simplicity: "Lo manejás desde el celular".
Reliability: "No se cae nunca, usamos tecnología de Google y Vercel".
Affordability: "Escalable: empezás con lo básico y crecemos juntos".
